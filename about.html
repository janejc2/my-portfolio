Hi, I'm Jane!

I was born in Johannesburg, South Africa, raised in sunny Phoenix, Arizona, and am currently living in Brooklyn, New York. I'm a lifelong artist and lover of all things creative.

My childhood love of drawing and painting led me to a degree in Industrial Design, where I developed an interest in human factors, user interaction, and problem-solving. I've worked in multidisciplinary roles in visual, brand, and UI/UX design, across agencies, in-house teams, and various industries.

Outside of work, you can find me at a concert, baking sourdough, trying out a new recipe, or exploring new restaurants, and coffee shops, and events in the city.
