Project Overview

The Community

Mission Village is a 55+ community in Riverside, California. The community offers exceptional amenities with a welcoming neighborhood atmosphere for an ideal California lifestyle.

The Brief

The client wanted a more modern brand to set them apart from other senior living communities. They wanted their new brand identity to be modern, relaxing, elegant, stylish, and traditional, and to be representative of their location in the California desert.
